## üí≥ Payment & Financial Features

### 1. **USSD Payment Integration** ‚≠ê HIGH PRIORITY
- **Why:** USSD is extremely popular in Nigeria, especially for users without smartphones or data
- **Implementation:**
  - Integrate with Paystack/Flutterwave USSD codes
  - Generate unique USSD codes for each transaction
  - SMS confirmation after payment
  - Support major banks (GTB, Access, UBA, First Bank, etc.)
- **User Flow:** Select \"Pay via USSD\" ‚Üí Receive code ‚Üí Dial code ‚Üí Confirm payment
- **Value:** Reach unbanked/underbanked users, increase payment success rate

### 2. **Bank Transfer with Auto-Verification**
- **Why:** Bank transfers are the most trusted payment method in Nigeria
- **Implementation:**
  - Generate unique reference codes for each payment
  - Integration with account verification APIs (Paystack, Flutterwave)
  - Auto-detect payments via webhook
  - Manual verification option for edge cases
- **Value:** Trust, familiarity, lower transaction fees

### 3. **Mobile Money Integration**
- **Why:** Growing mobile money adoption (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, etc.)
- **Implementation:**
  - Partner with mobile money providers
  - Direct wallet-to-wallet transfers

- **Value:** Alternative payment method, lower costs

### 4. **Payment Plans & Installments**
- **Why:** High rent amounts, irregular income patterns common in Nigeria
- **Implementation:**
  - Monthly, quarterly, semi-annual payment plans
  - Automatic reminders via SMS/WhatsApp
  - Late fee calculation with grace periods

- **Value:** Affordability, reduced defaults, higher occupancy

### 5. **Guarantor System**

### üü¢ High Priority

#### 1. Rent Savings Wallet & Goal Tracker
- **Priority:** High  
- **Where in app:**  
  - Frontend: Tenant `Profile` ‚Üí `Payments` / `Wallet` section (e.g. `pages/tenant/payments`), with summary tiles on `Home` and `My Bookings`.  
  - Backend: Extend `payments`/`wallet` modules (savings goals, locked balances, transaction history).  
- **Reason:** Many Nigerians struggle to accumulate lump-sum rent; a structured savings flow directly tackles this pain.  
- **Expected impact:** Higher conversion from ‚Äúinterested‚Äù to ‚Äúcan actually pay‚Äù; stickier tenants who log in regularly to track savings.

#### 2. Shared Rent / Roommate Matching
- **Priority:** High  
- **Where in app:**  
  - Frontend: New `Find Roommate` flow under `Services` or `Home` quick actions; filters surfaced on `Properties` page (\"room-share available\").  
  - Backend: Matching service under `modules/properties` + a small `modules/matching` service for roommate profiles and preferences.  
- **Reason:** Shared apartments are common in Lagos/Abuja; most people currently use informal WhatsApp/Telegram groups.  
- **Expected impact:** New demand segment (students, junior staff), better occupancy for larger units, more network effects inside the platform.

#### 3. Transport-Aware Search
- **Priority:** High  
- **Where in app:**  
  - Frontend: Extra filters on `Properties` search (\"Max commute time to VI/Ikeja/CMS\"), map overlays; a ‚ÄúCommute‚Äù tab on property details.  
  - Backend: Integration layer in `infrastructure/maps` to estimate commute times and typical costs using mapping APIs.  
- **Reason:** Lagos traffic is brutal; commute time is often more important than raw distance.  
- **Expected impact:** Differentiated search experience, higher search satisfaction, better match quality ‚Üí fewer failed viewings.

#### 4. Tenant Scoring & Portfolio Risk Dashboard
- **Priority:** High (for B2B/professional landlords)  
- **Where in app:**  
  - Frontend: Landlord `Dashboard` ‚Üí `Risk` or `Portfolio Health` widget, plus risk badges on the `Applications` list.  
  - Backend: Scoring service in `modules/landlord` or `modules/risk` using payment history, disputes, and background check status.  
- **Reason:** Professional landlords think in terms of portfolio risk, not just single tenants.  
- **Expected impact:** Better pricing and approval decisions; strong selling point to serious landlords and PM firms.

#### 5. SLA Dashboard for Maintenance & Support
- **Priority:** High  
- **Where in app:**  
  - Frontend: Landlord & Property Manager dashboards ‚Äì SLA cards (avg response time, % completed within X hours) embedded in `maintenance` views.
